About Us

We want to eliminate the bias from movie reviews...



Have you ever been disappointed after watching a movie that had high reviews by critics?
Or absolutely enjoyed a great movie even though critics gave it a poor review? This is because the critics are biased to certain genres, directors, casts e.t.c. that do not necessarily match yours.

We have felt those pains too, that’s why we’ve introduced MovieSolsys to eliminate this bias!
